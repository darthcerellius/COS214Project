\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Observes the \mbox{\hyperlink{classRealRaceTrack}{Real\+Race\+Track}} position and records it when notified of a position change. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classRacingCommand}{Racing\+Command}} uses this object to read the state of the race track after a qualifying or real race occurs.
