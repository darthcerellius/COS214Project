Class to construct a race weekend. 

The race weekend class holds the details of each a track that will be used for a race. It includes the track name, location (continent) as well as the length of a track in km. The date and weather conditions for the track is stored in structs. struct that holds date of the race weekend d = day of date m = month of date year = year of date
