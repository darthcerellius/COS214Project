\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The abstract \mbox{\hyperlink{classInstruction}{Instruction}} class acts as an interface for the concrete commands. These commands are invoked by the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPitCrew}{Pit\+Crew}} and execute instructions in the Car class. \mbox{\hyperlink{classPitCrew}{Pit\+Crew}} will send a command to the Car through one of the child classes\+: \mbox{\hyperlink{classTacticalDecision}{Tactical\+Decision}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{classEventResponse}{Event\+Response}}. Acts as the \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommand}{Command}} participants in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classInstruction}{Instruction}} \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommand}{Command}} pattern.
